Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Clipper Construction, LLC ("Company," "we," "us") operates the Clipper Command Terminal application ("Application"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Application, we may collect the following types of information:

Account Information: Your email address used for authentication via magic link login.

Third-Party Service Data: When you connect Procore or QuickBooks Online, we access project data, financial records, invoices, bills, bank balances, and other business information from those services on your behalf. This data is retrieved using OAuth 2.0 authorization tokens that you explicitly grant.

Usage Data: Basic usage logs and error reports to maintain and improve the Application.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to: (a) provide and operate the Application's dashboard and reporting features; (b) sync and display project and financial data from connected services; (c) authenticate authorized users; and (d) maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Application.

3. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security policies. OAuth tokens for third-party services are stored encrypted. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with Procore and Intuit QuickBooks Online. When you connect these services, we access data through their official APIs using OAuth 2.0 tokens you authorize. We do not sell, share, or disclose your third-party service data to any parties outside the Company except as required to operate the Application. Your use of third-party services is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain synced data for as long as your account is active and the third-party services remain connected. When you disconnect a service, the associated OAuth tokens are deleted. You may request deletion of all your data by contacting us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or business data. We may share information only: (a) with your consent; (b) to comply with legal obligations; or (c) to protect the rights and safety of the Company or its users.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: (a) access the data we hold about you; (b) request correction of inaccurate data; (c) request deletion of your data; (d) disconnect third-party services at any time through the Application's Settings page; and (e) revoke OAuth access directly through Procore or QuickBooks.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Application uses essential cookies and local storage for authentication session management only. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
